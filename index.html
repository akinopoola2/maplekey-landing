The platform

Three portals, one platform.

MapleKey is built around the reality that tenants, property managers, and owners each need different tools. Not a repurposed landlord app. A proper system for all three, in every country we expand to.

01 · For Tenants

Report, pay, renew.

A clean mobile portal where tenants handle everything without phone calls or paperwork.

  • Photo issue reporting with AI triage
  • Rent payment (Interac, card, bank)
  • Digital lease signing and renewals
  • Secure document vault
02 · For Managers

Run your portfolio.

The engine room. Triage tickets, post rent, generate compliant notices, manage multiple properties with confidence.

  • AI-assisted maintenance triage
  • Jurisdiction-aware notice templates
  • Rent ledger with auto-reconciliation
  • Photo-logged inspections
03 · For Owners

Stay informed.

Owner clients see what matters without the noise. Clean statements, real-time occupancy, tax-ready exports.

  • AI-generated plain-language summaries
  • Occupancy and rent roll dashboards
  • Maintenance spend tracking
  • Annual tax export (CRA T776 and more)
Intelligence, by design

Intelligence built in, not bolted on.

Most property management software added AI as a chatbot afterthought. MapleKey is architected around AI from day one, solving the specific pains of running properties: maintenance triage, regulatory compliance, and portfolio intelligence.

⚡

Instant Maintenance Triage

Tenant uploads a photo and a short description. AI categorizes the issue, estimates urgency, suggests the right trade, and drafts a work order in seconds.

Tenant photo of a dripping faucet: categorized as plumbing · non-urgent · supply valve likely · work order drafted.
⚖

Tenancy Compliance Assistant

Ask questions in plain English or French. Generate compliant notices for your jurisdiction: Manitoba RTB at launch, every province and country as we expand.

"Draft a notice to terminate for Unit 304, valid under Manitoba RTB rules" · compliant form, correct timeline, bilingual output.

Your Property Brain

Ask anything about any property in your portfolio. Maintenance history, tenant patterns, financial trends. Years of data answered in one sentence.

"Which properties had the highest maintenance costs last year and why?" · portfolio-wide analysis in under 5 seconds.
