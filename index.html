What we're building

Three portals, one platform.

MapleKey is designed around the reality that tenants, property managers, and owners each need different tools. Not a repurposed landlord app. A proper system for all three.

01 · For Tenants

Report, pay, renew.

A clean mobile-friendly portal where tenants handle everything without phone calls or paperwork.

  • Photo issue reporting
  • Rent payment + Interac e-Transfer
  • Lease signing + renewals
  • Document vault
02 · For Managers

Run your portfolio.

The engine room. Triage tickets, post rent, generate compliant notices, and keep multiple properties organized.

  • Maintenance ticket triage
  • Manitoba RTA notice templates
  • Rent ledger + reconciliation
  • Inspections with photo logs
03 · For Owners

Stay informed.

Your client investors see what matters without the noise. Clean statements, real-time occupancy, tax-ready exports.

  • Monthly owner statements
  • Occupancy + rent roll
  • Maintenance spend tracking
  • CRA T776 annual export
